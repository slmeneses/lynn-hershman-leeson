is widely recognized for her innovative work that confronts where we are in society and investigates issues including: the relationship between humans and technology, identity, surveillance, and the use of media as a tool of empowerment against censorship and political repression.

hover for knowledge

As of late, many of her works have been driven by the neighborhing technology-heavy region of Silicon Valley and the fast paced development of artificial intelligence.

She has collaborated with notable names in film including actress Tilda Swinton, and film director and screenwriter Eleanor Coppola, to create some of her most famous works.

She is from Cleveland, Ohio and came to the Bay Area in the 70’s. She went to graduate school at UC Berkeley, where she presented her first pieces of art. She has spent most of her life in San Francisco since.

Her artwork has been featured in cities across the nation and many public collections including spaces at The San Francisco Museum of Modern Art and ​​The Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco (de Young).
